Privacy choices

Your data, your control.

AfterBuy does not use accounts in the MVP, so most data is tied to your local app install or anonymous device identifier instead of a user profile.

Requests you can make

  • Ask what data may be associated with your app install or anonymous device identifier.
  • Request deletion of backend data that can reasonably be matched to your app install or request details.
  • Correct inaccurate support or contact information you previously sent to us.
  • Ask questions about App Store privacy labels.

How to make a request

Email marcusperduee@gmail.com with the subject line "AfterBuy Privacy Request". Include your approximate request date, product searched, and any anonymous device ID shown in the app settings if available.

Because AfterBuy does not require accounts, we may be unable to identify backend records without enough request details. We will not ask for passwords because AfterBuy does not have user passwords.

In-app choices

  • Clear local search history in AfterBuy settings.
  • Turn off location permission in iOS Settings.
  • Use manual city or ZIP entry instead of precise location.
  • Reset the anonymous app/device identifier in AfterBuy settings when available.
