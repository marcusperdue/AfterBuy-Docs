Privacy Policy

AfterBuy is designed as an account-free purchase decision tool. We collect only the data needed to search products, compare pricing signals, protect the service, and improve recommendation quality.

Who operates AfterBuy

AfterBuy is owned and operated by MRCV Technologies LLC in the United States.

Contact: info@mrcv.net

Data we collect

Product searches Search terms, selected product names, product identifiers, product URLs when used, barcode values when used, and product metadata returned by data providers.
Price inputs The price you enter, product condition, and optional location context used to estimate nearby or pickup pricing.
Location Only if you allow it or manually enter a city/ZIP. Location is used to check local shopping context and is not required to use basic product search.
Anonymous device data An anonymous app install/device identifier, DeviceCheck/App Attest style integrity signals when available, request timestamps, rate-limit counters, and abuse-prevention metadata.
Diagnostics Crash, error, latency, provider-quality, rejected-listing, and server logs needed to operate and improve the app.
Local history Search and analysis history saved on your device so you can revisit previous recommendations.

How we use data

  • Find product matches and pricing signals.
  • Calculate a buy, wait, compare, or avoid recommendation.
  • Estimate confidence and detect bad, suspicious, or unrelated provider results.
  • Maintain local history on your device.
  • Prevent abuse, rate-limit expensive API calls, and protect API costs.
  • Debug errors, improve product matching, and improve pricing quality.

Data sharing

AfterBuy does not sell personal data and does not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

To provide product and price results, AfterBuy may send product search terms, product identifiers, general location context, and request metadata to service providers such as hosting, database, cache, product search, product data, or analytics/diagnostic infrastructure. These providers are used to operate AfterBuy, not to sell data.

Accounts and payments

AfterBuy does not require an account. Optional AfterBuy Plus subscriptions are handled through Apple In-App Purchase. Apple processes payment information under Apple's policies; AfterBuy receives subscription transaction identifiers, product identifiers, status, trial state, environment, and expiration or revocation dates needed to verify access and enforce usage limits. AfterBuy does not receive your full payment-card details.

Retention

Local history remains on your device until you delete it or clear app data. Backend analysis records and provider-quality logs may be retained to operate the service, support rate limits, improve pricing quality, and build anonymous historical pricing intelligence. We may delete or de-identify records when they are no longer needed.

Your choices

  • You can use AfterBuy without creating an account.
  • You can deny location permission or use manual location entry.
  • You can clear local history in the app.
  • You can reset the anonymous app/device identifier from the app when available.
  • You can request access, deletion, or correction through Privacy Choices.

Children

AfterBuy is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

AfterBuy uses HTTPS for backend communication and keeps provider API keys on the backend. No security method is perfect, but the app is designed to avoid accounts and passwords to reduce user risk.

Changes

We may update this policy as AfterBuy changes. When we do, we will update the effective date on this page.
