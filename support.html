GitHub Issues: AfterBuy-Docs Issues

Do not post private information, API keys, payment data, exact home addresses, or sensitive screenshots in a public GitHub issue. Use email for anything private.

What to include

  • Your device model and iOS version.
  • The product you searched for.
  • The price and condition you entered.
  • Whether the issue happened on Wi-Fi or cellular.
  • A screenshot if it does not include sensitive information.

Product and pricing notes

AfterBuy gives purchase guidance based on available product and pricing signals. Final product price, tax, shipping, pickup availability, return policy, and seller condition should always be confirmed with the retailer or seller before buying.
